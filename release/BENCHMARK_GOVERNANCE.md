# CIGCS Benchmark Governance

Version: v1.0.0

This repository defines the frozen CIGCS constitutional benchmark suite.

## Governance Status

The benchmark contains four immutable constitutional proof volumes:

- Volume I: Constitutional Admissibility Corpus
- Volume II: Safety Predicate Corpus
- Volume III: Render Authorization Artifact Corpus
- Volume IV: Constitutional Enforcement Corpus

## Benchmark Rule

No new proof categories may be added to v1.0.0.

No case renumbering is permitted.

No appended cases such as CB-100A, SB-100A, RAA-100A, or CE-100A are permitted.

Corrections may be made only for documented defects.

## Canonical Corpus Size

Total executable constitutional proofs: 400

## Execution Requirement

A conforming implementation must execute the full corpus and produce:

- deterministic results
- expected match count equal to total case count
- zero expected mismatches
- hash-bound evidence output
- benchmark metrics
